Controls
 
Use the arrow keys or WASD to move,
Space to fire,
and N to detonate a nuke.


Objective
 
Shoot the raiders
to prevent them stealing habitats
and transforming to marauders.
Bursters and minelayers
appear at higher levels.
Bursters explode into chasers when shot.
Harriers will appear after a time.


Scoring
 
Raiders:     150
Marauders:   150
Harriers:    200
Minelayers:  250
Bursters:   1000
Chasers:     150
