Controls
 
Use the arrow keys
or WASD to move,
Space to fire,
and N to detonate a nuke.


Objective
 
Shoot the raiders to prevent
them from stealing habitats
and transforming to marauders.

Bursters and minelayers
appear at higher levels.

Bursters explode into chasers
when shot.

Harriers will appear after a time.


Scoring
 
Raider     150
Marauder   150
Harrier    200
Minelayer  250
Burster   1000
Chaser     150
